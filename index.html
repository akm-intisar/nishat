Massachusetts Institute of Technology, edX

MIT’s Department of Economics and the Abdul Latif Jameel Poverty Action Lab (J-PAL) designed the MicroMasters® program credential in Data, Economics, and Development Policy (DEDP) to equip learners with the practical skills and theoretical knowledge to tackle some of the most pressing challenges facing developing countries and the world’s poor. Research Interest

Social Equity

In the development of public policy, delivery of public services, enforcement of public policy, and administration of all agencies representing the public personally or by contract, social equity is the constructive contribution to fairness, justice, and equality.

Poverty Alleviation

Poverty mitigation, also known as poverty relief or alleviation, is a series of economic and social interventions aimed at permanently lifting people out of poverty.

Planning

Planning is a global endeavor to systematically plan for the future in such a manner that our children and grandchildren can have happier lives and a better climate.

Public Policy

A course of action established and/or implemented by a government in reaction to public, real-world issues is known as public policy. Public policy has been described in a number of ways within this general definition.

Turning Evidence into Policy

One way to boost policy formulation is to use an evidence-based approach. It is based on the assumption that if the mechanism is driven by solid facts, better decisions will be taken.

Transporation
